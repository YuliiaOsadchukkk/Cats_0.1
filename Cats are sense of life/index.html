Why cats are sense of life?

Cats are the cutiest creatures on this planet. What with their little button noses that don’t mind being “booped”, big beautiful eyes that even look adorable when they are sleeping, and those dainty little paws that tap you ever so gently to get your attention. Other than their obvious good looks, another reason why cat lovers prefer them over dogs is the calm, dignified vibes they give off. When they snuggle next to you, or on top of you, they force you to stop and relax with them. It is a great feeling!
